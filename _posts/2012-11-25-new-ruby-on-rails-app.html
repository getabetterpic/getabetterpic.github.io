I walked through a few tutorials on RoR, but anyone who has done any programming knows you don't really "learn" a language until you build your own app and have to do some troubleshooting. So I took a stab at it and created an invoicing app designed with law firms in mind. You can take a stroll through it at invoiceapp.getabetterpic.com if you care to. Let me know if you have suggestions for usability changes, or any other suggestions for that matter. Also, if it is down, try it again later. I am currently hosting it on a personal machine until I'm ready to spend money on a server to host it.
