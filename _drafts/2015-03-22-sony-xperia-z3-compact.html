--- layout: post title: Sony Xperia Z3 Compact date: '2015-03-22T10:15:00.000-04:00' author: Daniel Smith tags: modified_time: '2015-03-22T10:15:05.714-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-358950712929443967.post-322192081487726647 blogger_orig_url: http://www.getabetterpic.com/2015/03/sony-xperia-z3-compact.html --- I recently upgraded my phone to the Sony Xperia Z3 Compact. This is coming from a Moto X (1st gen.). So far I really like the phone. One of the killer features on this is it is waterproof. I plan on taking it with me to the pool this summer to capture moments with my son. Think underwater video.

The phone itself is beautiful, reminiscent of an iPhone 4, with a glass back, metal around the edges (although those are covered in a clear silicone-like plastic with flaps that lift for things like SIM and microSD access).
