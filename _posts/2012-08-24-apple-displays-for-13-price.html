--- layout: post title: Apple Displays for 1/3 the price? date: '2012-08-24T14:03:00.002-04:00' author: Daniel Smith tags: - monitor - '27' - display - maximum pc - 2560x1440 - apple modified_time: '2012-08-24T14:03:39.738-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-358950712929443967.post-5179662875002077936 blogger_orig_url: http://www.getabetterpic.com/2012/08/apple-displays-for-13-price.html --- So I was reading through the latest issue of Maximum PC and apparently there are 27" monitors shipping from South Korea that use the same panels Apple's revered displays use, but at about a third the cost. They run the same 2560x1440 resolution, but have cheaper stands and no on screen display. I just recently bought a new 24" monitor, and as soon as I saw these, I thought "Why didn't I know about this sooner?!?" But then my next thought was "What would I do with a 2560x1440 resolution?" Which led to the age-old geek answer, "I don't know, but I still want one."
